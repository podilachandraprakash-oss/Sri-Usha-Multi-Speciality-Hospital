Healthcare Services

Explore our premium medical, surgical, and therapeutic services designed for patient recovery.

General Medicine

Dedicated consultations for adult systemic conditions. Fully equipped for emergency medical cases including fits, accidents, paralysis, chest pain, and breathing issues (asthma, COPD, severe cold, and cough).

Dental Care

Premium dental services featuring state-of-the-art dental laser treatments, advanced implant care, painless root canals (RCT), wire aligners, wisdom tooth extractions, and cosmetic smile designs.

General Surgery

Comprehensive open surgical interventions in modular theatres, covering hernia repairs, appendicitis, thyroidectomy, lump removals, and diverse soft-tissue surgeries.

Laparoscopic Surgery

Advanced minimally invasive keyhole procedures with specifications including Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy (gallbladder), Laparoscopic Appendectomy, Hernia repair, and Hysterectomy for less pain and faster recovery.

Dialysis Services

Advanced renal dialysis systems staffed by experienced technicians to monitor patients with chronic kidney failure under safe sterile layouts.

Biochemistry & Microbiology Lab

Advanced pathological laboratory featuring fully automated biochemistry analyzers and microbiology divisions for hormone assays, culture and sensitivity tests, blood profiles, and rapid, validated diagnostics.

24x7 Pharmacy

In-house automated pharmacy stocked with authentic pharmaceuticals, vaccines, surgical disposables, pediatric preparations, and emergency medications.

Emergency Care

Immediate therapeutic support for medical emergencies, acute trauma, poisoning, cardiac instability, and respiratory failures with qualified duty doctors on active alert.

Health Checkups

Preventive clinical screening modules including corporate health reviews, geriatric packages, diabetic screenings, and comprehensive full-body checks.

Patient Consultation

Detailed outpatient consulting suites where patients receive long-term therapy guidance, specialist diagnoses, and customized health recovery plans.

X-Ray Services

High-resolution digital X-ray diagnostic imaging providing immediate skeletal, chest, and emergency trauma scans with minimal radiation exposure.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Dedicated multi-bed ICU providing 24/7 continuous patient monitoring, critical care medical staffing, and advanced hemodynamic support for acute conditions.

Gynaecology & Obstetrics

Expert women's health services including prenatal & postnatal care, normal and caesarean deliveries, gynecological screenings, and general maternal wellness.

Anaesthesiology

Comprehensive perioperative care, surgical anaesthesia administration, local block setups, pain management consultations, and critical resuscitation support.

Ventilator & Central Oxygen

High-end mechanical ventilator setups and central oxygen pipeline distribution to ensure immediate life-saving respiratory support in emergency and ICU suites.

ECG & Cardiac Screening

Immediate Electrocardiogram (ECG) scanning services to track cardiac rhythms, detect arrhythmias, and support rapid evaluation in acute chest pain or routine checks.
