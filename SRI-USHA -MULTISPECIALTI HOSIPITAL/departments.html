Medical Departments

Explore our organized medical disciplines staffed by licensed clinicians.

General Medicine

Treating systemic adult ailments and chronic diseases. Fully equipped to manage acute emergencies like fits (seizures), accidents, paralysis (stroke), chest pain, and breathing issues (asthma, cold, cough).

  • Lead: Dr. A. Venkanna, MBBS, MD (Practice since 2013)
  • Timings: Mon-Sun, 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Specialty: Fits, Accidents, Paralysis & Chest Pain

Dentistry

Comprehensive dental surgery, painless root canals (RCT), wire aligners, wisdom tooth extractions, advanced dental laser treatments, and specialized implant care.

  • Lead: Dr. A. Usharani, BDS (Practice since 2014)
  • Timings: Mon-Sat, 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Specialty: Laser Treatment & Advanced Implant Care

General Surgery

Modular surgical theatres delivering comprehensive open surgeries, hernia repairs, appendicitis, thyroidectomy, lump removals, and minor/major soft-tissue procedures.

  • Lead: Consulting Surgical Specialists
  • Timings: By Scheduled Booking
  • Specialty: Open & General Surgical Care

Laparoscopic Surgery

High-end minimally invasive keyhole operations with specifications including Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy (gallbladder), Laparoscopic Appendectomy, Hernia repair, and Hysterectomy for minimal pain and faster recovery.

  • Lead: Certified Laparoscopic Surgeons
  • Timings: Scheduled & Emergency
  • Specialty: Keyhole Minimally Invasive Surgery

Gynaecology & Obstetrics

Comprehensive maternal health services covering prenatal & postnatal care, normal and caesarean deliveries, gynecological screenings, and general women's wellness.

  • Lead: Consulting Gynaecologists
  • Timings: Mon-Sat (Scheduled OPD)
  • Specialty: Maternity, Delivery & Women's Health

Nephrology & Dialysis

Advanced dialysis tables operating under sterile setups to filter blood for patients with kidney disorders.

  • Support: Renal Dialysis Technicians
  • Timings: Scheduled Sessions
  • Specialty: Hemodialysis & Kidney Care

Biochemistry & Microbiology Lab

Fully automated biochemistry analyzers, hormone assays, microbiological cultures, sensitivity tests, blood profiles, and infectious disease diagnostics with rapid, accurate reports.

  • Support: Certified Lab Pathologists
  • Timings: Active 24 Hours / 7 Days
  • Specialty: Pathology & Culturing

Emergency, Trauma & ICU

Equipped trauma chambers and a multi-bed ICU supported by central oxygen piping lines, mechanical ventilators, and immediate cardiac resuscitation sets.

  • Support: 24x7 Critical Care Officers & Nurses
  • Timings: Active 24 Hours / 7 Days
  • Specialty: Ventilator Support, ICU & Triage

Diagnostics & 24x7 Pharmacy

High-resolution digital X-rays, 12-lead ECG cardiac scans, and an in-house automated pharmacy stocked with authentic pharmaceuticals.

  • Support: Radiologists & Pharmacists
  • Timings: Active 24 Hours / 7 Days
  • Specialty: Lab, ECG, X-Ray & Medicines
