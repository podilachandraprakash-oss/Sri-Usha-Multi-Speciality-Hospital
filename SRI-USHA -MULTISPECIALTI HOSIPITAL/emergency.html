Emergency Checklist

  • Stay Calm: Maintain calmness to instruct medical crews accurately.
  • Locate: Identify landmark directions for the incoming ambulance squad.
  • Gather Records: Put patient ID documents and past prescription logs in a bag.
  • Triage: Follow on-phone directions given by our emergency officer.

Clinical Stabilization & ICU Systems

Our trauma care units and fully functional ICU are equipped with modern resuscitation sets including mechanical ventilators, automated external defibrillators (AEDs), 12-lead ECG setups, and centralized oxygen pipeline feeds.

Experienced critical care clinicians and senior nursing staff remain on active rotation 24 hours a day to provide patient monitoring and immediate life-support services upon arrival at our campus.

Sri Usha Multi Speciality Hospital is a premier healthcare institution providing exceptional general medicine, dental care, dialysis, laparoscopy, and emergency clinical services to Kothagudem and the surrounding regions.
