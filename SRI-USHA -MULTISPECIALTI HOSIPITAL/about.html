About Our Hospital

Serving Kothagudem with dedicated medical professionals and luxury medical facilities.

Dedicated to Your Healing & Comfort

Sri Usha Multi Speciality Hospital was established on 13th June 2022 in Kothagudem, Telangana with a clear vision: to make advanced multispeciality healthcare accessible, high-quality, and highly compassionate.

Under the leadership of our core clinicians, Dr. A. Venkanna (MBBS, MD) specializing in General Medicine, and Dr. A. Usharani (BDS) specializing in Dental Surgery, our hospital has grown to become a cornerstone of the regional healthcare system.

We house a modern 20-bed hospital setup with specialized modular infrastructure, including dialysis facilities, laparoscopy setups, a 24x7 diagnostics lab, and an in-house pharmacy, eliminating the need for patients to travel to larger cities for critical healthcare diagnostics and treatments.

Our Core Foundations

  • Regulated Hygiene: Daily automated sanitization checks to match clinical safety standards.
  • Patient-First Ethics: Transparent OPD billings and detailed consultation outlines.
  • Comprehensive Care: A 20-bed ward, pharmacy, laboratory diagnostics, and clinical suites inside a single campus.
  • Insurance Facilities: Aditya Birla Health/Life Insurance and Galaxy Health Insurance cashless facilities are active for hassle-free treatment.

Patient-Centric Treatment Philosophy

We believe clinical rehabilitation is most effective in environments that feel secure and clean. Therefore, our patient care philosophy shifts away from standard sterile clinical systems toward human-centered hospitality.

From specialized single room amenities to custom dietitian-approved food plans, we verify every parameter is aligned for comfort during healing. Our diagnostics are run with prompt triage algorithms so emergency critical support reaches patients instantly.
